Reviewer Matchmaking Survey

Welcome to DA-IICT portal for Reviewer Matchmaking survery. It is a part of a research problem of how to assign a manuscript to its best-matching reviewer in the peer review process of a conference. We are in the process of developing an AI-based reviewer match-making system that aims to optimize the allocation of manuscripts submitted in a conference to review.

Your contribution to this survey will help us build better AI matchmaking systems 🤖. It won't take more than 5 minutes 😄. Please select your name from the drop-down menu below to proceed further. It will enable us fetch data corresponding to you.


This data is solely for research purpose. Your identity will not be disclosed. You may enlist yourself as a part of contributors list when the dataset will be made public by clicking on the radio button below.
